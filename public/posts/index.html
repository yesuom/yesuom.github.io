LIF Neuron Model

Here is a note about Leaky Integrate-and-Fire (LIF) model. The Equation The dynamics of the membrane potential $V(t)$ is described by: $$ \tau_m \frac{dV}{dt} = -(V(t) - V_{rest}) + R \cdot I(t) $$Where: $\tau_m$ is the membrane time constant. $I(t)$ is the input current.
